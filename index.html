Frequently Asked Questions

Will the videos look like "cheap AI"? +
No. We combine the absolute best AI generators (Runway Gen-3, Midjourney, HeyGen) with expert human editors, professional typography, sound effects, and color grading. The final output is cinematic and optimized for consumer trust.
How do you handle physical products? +
You provide us with high-resolution images or raw phone footage of your product, and our AI pipeline integrates them into photorealistic, dynamically rendered scenes featuring digital models using your items.
How long does the production process take? +
Typically, scripts are ready for review in 48 hours. After approval, the AI assets are generated and edited, with first drafts delivered in 3 to 5 business days.
Can I clone my own voice or create an executive avatar? +
Yes. Under the Enterprise plan, we record a short reference sample of you or your team members to generate a highly accurate voice clone and a digital avatar capable of reading any script.
Are revisions included in my retainer? +
Yes. Every plan includes dedicated revision cycles. We adjust script copy, voice tone, B-roll timing, and subtitle layout until it meets your brand's standards.
