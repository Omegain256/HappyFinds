Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to HappyFinds. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data of our website visitors, clients, and partners. This policy details how we gather, process, and protect your information when utilizing our AI video generation and rendering platforms.

2. The Data We Collect

We may collect, store, and process several categories of information, including:

• Identity and Contact Data: Name, work email address, company website, and brand details provided during consultation bookings.

• Visual and Auditory Creative Assets: Product photos, raw brand videos, logos, and audio samples provided to build your AI rendering assets and custom voice models.

• Technical Usage Data: IP addresses, browser types, session durations, and interaction records while navigating happyfinds.co.ke.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly to provide AI video rendering services, including:

• Drafting and editing custom video script concepts for your review.

• Generating digital actor visual graphics and custom speech synthetics.

• Managing client communication and scheduling onboarding demonstrations.

• Improving website performance, responsiveness, and aesthetic rendering.

4. Voice Cloning and Identity Policy

We strictly require explicit written consent from the target individual before cloning any human voice or constructing high-fidelity digital twin avatars. We never utilize voice clones or avatar assets for unauthorized distributions.

5. Contact Details

If you have any inquiries regarding this Privacy Policy or wish to modify your stored creative assets, please email us directly at hello@happyfinds.co.ke.
